Alternatively "Your Climb". Still being worked on, a more diaglogue heavy game about people climbimg a tower in search of the ethereal 
'Top'. Focuses on character interaction and a more subtle style of story telling, intended to have multiple endings based on how players resolve conflicts or offer advice to the other 'Climbers'

Controls -
WASD or Direction keys to move
Mouse to look and select dialogue
E to interact/talk
